The Brave Warrior of the Sea

Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates and the son of Yasopp, one of Shanks' top marksmen. Despite being the crew's self-described coward, Usopp has repeatedly faced impossible odds — his ingenuity, creativity, and legendary marksmanship saving the crew time and again. His dream is to become a brave warrior of the sea like his father.

"I am the great captain Usopp! I have 8,000 men under my command!"

Black Kabuto & Pop Greens

Usopp's custom-made sling shot, Black Kabuto, combined with his Pop Greens — living plant seeds that explode into enormous man-eating plants, needle barrages, or massive vines — make him a one-man army capable of controlling entire battlefields from range.

Observation Haki

Usopp unconsciously awakened his Observation Haki during the battle of Dressrosa, sensing Sugar's fear across an enormous distance and landing a godlike snipe. His Haki, while untrained, is naturally incredible — enough to be called "God Usopp" by the Dressrosa citizens.

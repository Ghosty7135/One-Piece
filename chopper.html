The Human Reindeer

Tony Tony Chopper is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates and a reindeer who ate the Hito Hito no Mi (Human-Human Fruit). Formerly an outcast on Drum Island, shunned by both humans and reindeer, he was taken in by the eccentric Dr. Hiriluk. His dream is to create a cure for all diseases in the world.

"I'm not a tanuki! I'm a reindeer! And I'm a doctor!"

Rumble Ball Mastery

Chopper developed the Rumble Ball, a drug that temporarily shifts his Zoan transformations into seven additional hybrid forms. He has since trained to access most of these forms without the drug, including the terrifying Monster Point which triples his size into a rampaging behemoth.

Medical Genius

Chopper is a prodigy doctor trained under Dr. Kureha, the best physician on Drum Island. He can diagnose any illness, perform complex surgeries in the field, and developed entirely new treatments during his two-year training under the World's Greatest Doctor, Vegapunk's mentor.

THE MENTOR'S LEGACY

Chopper carries the spirit of every doctor who believed in the miracle of medicine.

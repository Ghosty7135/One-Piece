The Fifth Emperor

Monkey D. Luffy is the main protagonist of One Piece and the Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. After his victory over Kaido, he was officially recognized as one of the Four Emperors of the Sea, known for bringing liberation to oppressed kingdoms.

"Being the Pirate King isn't about being the most powerful. It's about being the freest man in the world!"

Gear 5: Warrior of Liberation

The peak of Luffy's power, Gear 5 allows him to fight with total freedom, turning his surroundings into rubber and defying the laws of physics with "comic-like" physics. It is the awakening of the Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika.

Master of Haki

Luffy has mastered all three types of Haki: Conqueror's, Armament, and Observation, including their advanced "internal destruction" and "future sight" forms.

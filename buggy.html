YONKO

The World's Most "Successful" Pirate

Buggy the Genius Jester is the leader of the Cross Guild and one of the Four Emperors. Starting as an apprentice on Gol D. Roger's ship, Buggy has failed upwards through history with a combination of legendary luck, accidental associations, and a flair for showmanship that has convinced thousands of the world's most dangerous criminals to follow him.

"I'm going for the One Piece! Who's with me?!"

Leader of Cross Guild

In a shocking turn of events, Buggy became the figurehead for the Cross Guild, an organization that has placed bounties on Marine officers. While fans know he is technically "servicing" Dracule Mihawk and Sir Crocodile, the rest of the world sees him as the man who commands two of the most dangerous people on earth.

Bara Bara no Mi

Buggy possesses the Bara Bara no Mi (Chop-Chop Fruit), which allows him to split his body into pieces and control them independently. This makes him naturally immune to any cutting attacks, including those from the world's greatest swordsmen.

The Roger connection

Buggy lived his youth as an apprentice to the Pirate King, Gol D. Roger. He was "brothers" with Shanks and fought in battles against the legendary Whitebeard. His proximity to the world's greatest legends has granted him a status that far exceeds his actual combat prowess.

Legendary Charisma

Despite his cowardice and greed, Buggy has an accidental ability to inspire fanatical loyalty. His escape from Impel Down turned him into a messianic figure for hundreds of high-bounty prisoners, and his recent declaration to find the One Piece has ignited a fire in pirates across the globe.

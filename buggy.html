The World's Most "Successful" Pirate

Buggy the Genius Jester is the leader of the Cross Guild and one of the Four Emperors. Starting as an apprentice on Roger's ship alongside Shanks, he has famously "failed upwards" through history, gaining a massive following of Impel Down's most dangerous criminals.

"I'm going for the One Piece! Who's with me?!"

The Cross Guild

The figurehead for an organization that has turned the tide of the world by placing bounties on Marine officers. He "commands" two of the most dangerous pirates: Mihawk and Crocodile.

Bara Bara no Mi

His Chop-Chop Fruit makes him completely immune to all slicing attacks, allowing him to split his body into flying segments. He is the natural enemy of the world's greatest swordsmen.

SIGNATURE TECHNIQUES

Buggy's powers are a mix of devil fruit utility and "Special Buggy Balls."

Special Buggy Ball

A highly miniaturized yet incredibly powerful cannonball capable of destroying entire towns.

Bara Bara Festival

Splits his body into tiny pieces and sends them flying in all directions to overwhelm enemies.

Muggy Ball

Even more miniaturized than the Buggy Ball, hidden within his shoe for surprise explosions.

Airborne Slash Escape

Automatically separates his body when slashed, making him completely immune to sword attacks.

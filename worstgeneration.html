THE WORST GENERATION

ELEVEN SUPERNOVAS WHO SHOOK THE WORLD

The Worst Generation is a notorious group of twelve pirates who rose to infamy around the same era. Eleven of them, known as the Supernovas, arrived simultaneously at the Sabaody Archipelago — a lawless port before the Grand Line's final leg — each carrying bounties of over 100,000,000 Berries. The twelfth, Marshall D. Teach (Blackbeard), was added later. Their actions following the Summit War of Marineford — defeating two Emperors, dissolving entire crews, and reshaping the New World's power balance — have branded them as the most dangerous generation of pirates in history.

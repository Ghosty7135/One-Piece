REVOLUTION

Toppling the World Order

The Revolutionary Army is a powerful military organization that seeks to overthrow the World Government and their oppressive celestial dragons. Unlike pirates who seek personal treasure, the Revolutionaries fight for the liberation of nations and the restoration of freedom across the globe.

"There can be no happiness in a world where the unnecessary are tossed away."

Monkey D. Dragon

The Supreme Commander and the "World's Worst Criminal." Dragon is Luffy's father and the bridge to the greatest secrets of the world. He is a master strategist who possesses an unknown power involving wind and storms.

Sabo: Flame Emperor

The Revolutionary Army's Chief of Staff and Luffy's sworn brother. After inheriting the Flame-Flame Fruit from Ace, Sabo has become a symbol of rebellion, earning the title "Flame Emperor" after his infiltration of Mariejois.

Emporio Ivankov

The "Miracle Person" and commander of the G-Army. A master of the Horm-Horm Fruit, Ivankov leads the Newkama Land and serves as one of Dragon's closest and most loyal allies.

The Liberation of Nations

The Army works in the shadows, helping oppressed kingdoms rise up against their tyrants. Their network is vast, with commanders stationed in the North, South, East, West, and Grand Line divisions.

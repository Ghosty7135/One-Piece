REVOLUTION

Toppling the World Order

The Revolutionary Army is a powerful military organization that seeks to overthrow the World Government and their oppressive celestial dragons. Unlike pirates who seek personal treasure, the Revolutionaries fight for the liberation of nations and the restoration of freedom across the globe.

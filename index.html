The Epic Tale

Gol D. Roger was known as the "Pirate King," the strongest and most infamous being to have sailed the Grand Line. His last words before his death revealed the existence of the greatest treasure in the world, One Piece.

Monkey D. Luffy, a boy who defies standard definitions, embarks on a journey to find the treasure and become the next Pirate King.

Mysterious Powers

  • Devil Fruits: Fruits that grant powers but steal the ability to swim. Paramecia, Zoan, and Logia.
  • Haki: Spiritual energy manifested as Observation, Armament, and the rare Conqueror's Haki.

The Greatest Cartographer

Nami is the Navigator and cartographer of the Straw Hat Pirates. Her dream is to draw a complete map of the entire world. A former thief who stole from pirates to buy back her village from Arlong, she now fights to chart every ocean of the Grand Line using her genius intellect and mastery of meteorology.

"I'm not your navigator because I have to be. It's because I want to sail with you."

Clima-Tact Mastery

Nami wields the Sorcery Clima-Tact, an upgraded weapon crafted by Usopp and enhanced by the Weather Science of Weatheria. It can create thunder clouds, lightning bolts, tornadoes, mirages, and hard-light constructs — making her one of the most versatile fighters on the crew.

Meteorological Genius

Nami's most invaluable ability is her innate mastery of weather patterns. She can read environmental clues invisible to others, predict storms, and has twice saved the Thousand Sunny from catastrophic weather phenomena in the New World.

YONKO

The Original Emperor

Red-Haired Shanks is the captain of the Red Hair Pirates and one of the Four Emperors that rule over the New World. Known for his tremendous Haki mastery, he is the only Yonko who does not possess a Devil Fruit power. Shanks is a figure of immense respect and fear, having once been an apprentice on Gol D. Roger's ship alongside Buggy.

"I've come to end this war."

Master of Haki

Shanks is widely considered the most powerful Haki user currently active. His Conqueror's Haki is so potent it can physically damage surroundings and knock out seasoned veteran pirates effortlessly. He can also use "Observation Killing," a technique that prevents opponents from seeing the future.

Red Hair Pirates

Unlike other Yonko who command massive fleets, Shanks leads a core group of extremely high-level pirates. His crew—including Benn Beckman, Lucky Roux, and Yasopp—is noted by the Marines for having the highest average bounty and most balanced strength of any pirate crew.

Diplomat & Guardian

Shanks often acts as a balance in world power. He met with Whitebeard to warn him about Blackbeard, stopped Kaido from entering the Marineford War, and eventually ended that same war by simply arriving. He protects the "New Era" and waits for the right time for the world to change.

Connection to Luffy

Shanks is the man who inspired Luffy's dream. He sacrificed his left arm to save Luffy from a Sea King and entrusted him with his signature Straw Hat. He believes Luffy is the one Roger was waiting for and keeps a distant, proud eye on the boy's progress.

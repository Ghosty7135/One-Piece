The Original Emperor

Red-Haired Shanks is the captain of the Red Hair Pirates and one of the Four Emperors. Known for his tremendous Haki mastery, he is the only Yonko who does not possess a Devil Fruit power. He was once a member of the Roger Pirates.

"I've come to end this war."

Conqueror's Mastery

Considered the most powerful Haki user active. His Conqueror's Haki is so potent it can physically damage the surroundings and negate the Observation Haki of others (Killer of Observation Haki).

Gryphon Master

Using his legendary saber, Gryphon, Shanks can clash with the strongest beings in the world, once famously dueling with Dracule Mihawk on equal terms.

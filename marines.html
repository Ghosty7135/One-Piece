ABSOLUTE JUSTICE

Military Might of the World Government

The Marines are the World Government's primary sea-based military force, tasked with maintaining global law and order. Their reach extends to every corner of the Four Seas and the Grand Line. Under the banner of "Absolute Justice," they hunt down pirates and revolutionaries who threaten the stability of the world order.

ABSOLUTE JUSTICE

The philosophical backbone of the Marines. While many follow it with honor, others use it to justify extreme measures to maintain order, believing that no sacrifice is too great for the sake of global peace.

The Fleet Admiral

Sakazuki (Akainu): The current leader of the Marines. Known for his "Thorough Justice" and Magma-Magma Fruit powers, he has moved Marine Headquarters into the New World to directly challenge the Yonko era.

The Three Admirals

The Marines' greatest individual powers. Current Admirals include Kizaru (Light), Fujitora (Gravity), and Ryokugyu (Forest). They represent the pinnacle of military combat strength.

Marine Legends

Monkey D. Garp (The Hero) and Sengoku the Buddha. These veterans represent the previous era, having fought legendary battles against the Pirate King, Gol D. Roger, and Gold Lion Shiki.

Cipher Pol (CP0)

The World Government's secret intelligence agency. While not strictly Marines, they work in the shadows to handle assassinations and espionage that the public military cannot touch.

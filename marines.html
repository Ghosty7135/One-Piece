ABSOLUTE JUSTICE

Military Might of the World Government

The Marines are the World Government's primary sea-based military force, tasked with maintaining global law and order. Under the banner of "Absolute Justice," they hunt down pirates and revolutionaries who threaten stability.

ABSOLUTE JUSTICE

The philosophical backbone of the Marines. A belief that no sacrifice is too great for the sake of global peace.

The Fleet Admiral

Sakazuki (Akainu): The current leader, known for his "Thorough Justice" and Magma-Magma Fruit powers. He led the Marines to overwhelming victory at Marineford.

Great Eruption

The Three Admirals

Current Admirals include Kizaru (Light), Fujitora (Gravity), and Ryokugyu (Nature). They represent the pinnacle of military strength.

The Hero of the Marines

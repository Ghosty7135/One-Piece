The Sole Survivor of Ohara

Nico Robin is the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates and the only person in the world alive who can read the Poneglyphs — ancient stone tablets that contain the true history of the world. A former assassin for the criminal organization Baroque Works, she carries the will of Ohara and seeks to discover the Void Century and the True History of the World.

"I want to live! Take me out to sea with you!"

Hana Hana no Mi

Robin's Devil Fruit allows her to sprout copies of her body parts — arms, legs, eyes, even full bodies — on any surface within range. She uses this to restrain enemies with bone-crushing joint locks, create clones to attack from unexpected angles, or sprout wings of arms to fly.

Poneglyph Scholar

Robin is the only person in the world who can decipher Poneglyphs — the forbidden indestructible stone tablets scattered across the world. This ability makes her the most dangerous person to the World Government, earning her a bounty from childhood and the destruction of her entire island.

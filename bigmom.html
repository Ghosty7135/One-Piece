LEGACY YONKO

The Queen of Totto Land

Charlotte Linlin, better known as Big Mom, was the captain of the Big Mom Pirates and the only female member of the Four Emperors. She ruled over the massive archipelago of Totto Land with the goal of creating a utopia where all races could live together in harmony—under her absolute control. She is a powerhouse of raw strength and a master of life-manipulating abilities.

"LIFE... OR TREAT?!"

Soru Soru no Mi

Big Mom possessed the Soru Soru no Mi (Soul-Soul Fruit), which allowed her to manifest and manipulate human souls. She could draw out the life force of others or use her own soul to create "Homies"—sentient objects or phenomena like Prometheus (fire), Zeus/Napoleon (lightning), and Hera.

The Charlotte Family

Unlike any other pirate, Linlin built her crew through bloodline. She had 85 children from many different fathers of various races, making the Big Mom Pirates a family-based empire. Her children served as the backbone of her military and political power across the New World.

Totto Land Utopia

Linlin's dream was to have every race on earth sat at a dinner table as equals. While this sounds noble, her methods involved "Soul Taxing"—forcing citizens to give up a portion of their lifespans to live in her territory. She also suffered from "Hunger Pangs," during which she would destroy entire islands in search of a specific food.

Iron Balloon

Even without Devil Fruit powers, Linlin was a natural disaster. Born with skin so tough it was called an "Iron Balloon," she was strong enough to destroy a giant village as a mere child. Her Haki mastery and physical strength made her one of the most terrifying combatants in the history of the Grand Line.

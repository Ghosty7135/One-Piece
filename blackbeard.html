YONKO

The Admiral of the Blackbeard Pirates

Marshall D. Teach is the Admiral of the Blackbeard Pirates and one of the Four Emperors. He rose to power by betraying his former crew and harvesting some of the world's strongest Devil Fruits.

"PEOPLE'S DREAMS... HAVE NO END!"

The Duality of Power

The only person in history known to wield two Devil Fruit powers at once: the Yami Yami no Mi and the Gura Gura no Mi.

Abnormal Body

Teach possesses an "atypical body structure" that rumoredly never sleeps, allowing him to sustain multiple fruit powers.

YONKO

The Admiral of the Blackbeard Pirates

Marshall D. Teach, commonly known as Blackbeard, is the Admiral of the Blackbeard Pirates and one of the Four Emperors. He rose to power by betraying the Whitebeard Pirates, capturing Portgas D. Ace, and masterminding the greatest escape in Impel Down history. He is a calculating, patient, and ruthless predator who believes in fate and the power of dreams.

"PEOPLE'S DREAMS... HAVE NO END!"

The Duality of Power

Teach is the only person in history known to wield two Devil Fruit powers at once. He possesses the Yami Yami no Mi (Dark-Dark Fruit), which allows him to manipulate darkness and neutralize other fruit powers, and the Gura Gura no Mi (Tremor-Tremor Fruit), which he stole from Whitebeard to destroy the world with earthquakes.

Abnormal Body

According to Marco and others who knew him on Whitebeard's ship, Teach has an "atypical body structure" that never sleeps. This unique physiology is rumored to be the reason he can survive consuming more than one Devil Fruit, though the exact nature remains one of the world's biggest mysteries.

Calculated Rise

Everything Blackbeard does is part of a decades-long plan. He hid in the shadows of the Whitebeard Pirates for twenty years just to find the Dark-Dark Fruit. He used the Shichibukai position solely to gain access to Level 6 of Impel Down to recruit the world's most dangerous criminals for his crew.

Philosophy of Fate

Unlike many villains, Teach is a firm believer in destiny and dreams. He respects those with strong wills and isn't afraid to let luck play its part. However, his version of dreams involves chaos and the total destruction of the existing world order to usher in his own era.

The Governor-General

Kaido was the Governor-General of the Animal Kingdom Pirates and ruled over Wano Country with an iron fist. He was known as the "Strongest Creature in the World" and was famous for his inability to die, even during multiple execution attempts.

"Death is what completes a person!"

Mythical Zoan

The Uo Uo no Mi, Model: Seiryu allowed him to transform into a massive Azure Dragon, capable of wiping out mountains with his "Bolo Breath."

Absolute Durability

His skin was virtually impenetrable, requiring advanced applications of Armament Haki (Ryou) or Conqueror's Haki coating just to leave a scratch.

SIGNATURE TECHNIQUES

Kaido's combat style is a mix of pure brute force and elemental dragon powers.

Raimei Hakke

"Thunder Bagua"—a high-speed club strike coated in advanced Conqueror's Haki.

Boro Breath

A blast of heat from his dragon form that can vaporize entire mountains and castles.

Kaen Daiko

"Flaming Drum Dragon"—transforms his body into a giant lava-like dragon that melts anything it touches.

Tatsumaki Kaifu

"Dragon Twister Demolition Breath"—creates multiple tornadoes that fire sharp wind blades.

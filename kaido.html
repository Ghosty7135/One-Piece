LEGACY YONKO

The Strongest Creature

Kaido of the Beasts was the Governor-General of the Animal Kingdom Pirates and one of the Four Emperors. Known as the "Strongest Creature in the World," he ruled over the land of Wano with an iron fist for over 20 years. He is a massive, incredibly durable warrior whose only hobby was finding a way to successfully kill himself, as nothing in the world seemed strong enough to do it.

"Death is what completes a person!"

Uo Uo no Mi, Model: Seiryu

Kaido possessed the Mythical Zoan fruit that allowed him to transform into a high-scale Azure Dragon. In this form, he could fly using clouds, breathe massive blasts of fire (Bolo Breath), and manipulate weather elements like lightning and wind. His dragon scales granted him "absolute" durability.

Beasts Pirates

Kaido's military strength was built on the "Survival of the Fittest" philosophy. He aimed to create the most powerful army in the world, primarily consisting of Zoan-type Devil Fruit users, many of whom used artificial "SMILE" fruits. His Lead Performers—King, Queen, and Jack—were legendary disasters in their own right.

The Greatest War

Kaido's ultimate goal was to start the "Greatest War the world has ever seen" because he believed the current world was boring and only war could determine true value. He allied with Big Mom to form the most powerful pirate alliance in history before being challenged by the Ninja-Pirate-Mink-Samurai Alliance.

Fall of the Dragon

Kaido was finally defeated by Monkey D. Luffy on the roof of Onigashima. During this historic battle, Kaido pushed Luffy to awaken his "Joy Boy" form, Sun God Nika. Despite his villainy, Kaido stayed true to his warrior code, seeking a battle that would truly test his limits.

The Peak of the Great Pirate Era

Edward Newgate, better known as Whitebeard, was the captain of the Whitebeard Pirates and was widely considered the "Strongest Man in the World." He was the only pirate to ever truly rival the Pirate King Gol D. Roger in strength.

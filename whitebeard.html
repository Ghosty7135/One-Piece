LEGACY YONKO

The Strongest Man in the World

Edward Newgate, better known as Whitebeard, was the captain of the Whitebeard Pirates and was widely known as the "Strongest Man in the World" after the death of Gol D. Roger. He was one of the original Four Emperors and was the man closest to finding the One Piece. Unlike other pirates, his only true treasure was his "Family"â€”the crew he called his sons.

"ONE PIECE... IS REAL!"

Gura Gura no Mi

Whitebeard possessed the power of the Gura Gura no Mi, a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allowed him to create massive shockwaves through the air or land. These vibrations could trigger tsunamis and earthquakes, a power said to be capable of destroying the world itself.

The Whitebeard Family

Whitebeard's philosophy was unique: he didn't care for gold or fame. He used his position as a Yonko to create a gargantuan family. Every member of his crew, from the division commanders to the lowest deckhands, called him "Pops," and he would go to war with the entire world to protect a single one of them.

Marineford War

Whitebeard's final stand took place at Marineford, where he led his entire fleet to rescue his "son," Portgas D. Ace. Despite his old age and illness, he fought through thousands of Marines and three Admirals. He died standing up, with no wounds on his back, proving he never once turned his back on a fight.

The Final Legacy

With his dying breath, Whitebeard confirmed to the entire world that the One Piece truly exists, reigniting the Great Pirate Era Roger had started. His death marked the end of an era and shifted the balance of power in the New World forever.

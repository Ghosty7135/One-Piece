The Tenth Straw Hat

Jinbe is the helmsman of the Straw Hat Pirates — a whale shark Fish-Man and a master of Fish-Man Karate, the supreme martial art of the deep sea. A former Warlord of the Sea and ex-captain of the Sun Pirates, Jinbe is a man of immense honour who fought for human and Fish-Man equality before pledging his loyalty to Monkey D. Luffy.

"A person's blood is the same colour, no matter the race."

Fish-Man Karate

Jinbe is a grandmaster of Fish-Man Karate, a martial art that uses water as a medium for attacks. He can manipulate water molecules in the air and blood within living bodies, delivering devastating internal strikes. On the ocean, his power increases exponentially as he can weaponize the sea itself.

Supreme Helmsman

Jinbe's greatest skill aboard the Thousand Sunny is his unmatched helmsmanship. He can navigate through the most violent seas on pure instinct and physical mastery — single-handedly steering the crew through Big Mom's catastrophic Totto Land sea during the chaos of the Whole Cake Island escape.

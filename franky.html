The Dream Ship Builder

Franky is the shipwright of the Straw Hat Pirates and a human cyborg who rebuilt himself using the blueprints of Tom's Workers — the legendary shipbuilders who built the Pirate King's ship, the Oro Jackson. His dream is to build a ship that can circumnavigate the entire world, a dream he realised when he constructed the Thousand Sunny.

"A ship that can weather any sea and return its crew home safely — that's the mark of a true shipwright!"

Cyborg Body

After being struck by a sea train, Franky rebuilt himself into a cyborg using scrap metal and parts. He runs on cola, which he stores in his reinforced stomach. His body includes built-in weapons: cannons, laser beams, rocket launchers, and roller blades — all concealed within his hulking frame.

Vegapunk's Research

During the two-year time skip, Franky discovered the research lab of Dr. Vegapunk on Karakuri Island and absorbed all available data, massively upgrading himself into General Franky — capable of piloting a giant mech suit and firing devastating laser beams powered by cola.

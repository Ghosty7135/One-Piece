The Pirate Hunter

Roronoa Zoro is the combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates and the first member to join. A master of the unique Three Sword Style (Santoryu), his dream is to defeat Dracule Mihawk and become the World's Greatest Swordsman.

The Worst Generation

Zoro is one of the twelve pirates known as the Worst Generation (formerly the Eleven Supernovas). Like his captain Luffy, he arrived at Sabaody Archipelago with a bounty exceeding 100,000,000 Berries. His inclusion in this group highlights his immense threat level to the World Government — the only non-captain among the Supernovas, standing shoulder-to-shoulder with the most dangerous pirates of his era.

Santoryu Master

Zoro wields three swords simultaneously—one in each hand and one in his mouth. This unique style allows him to unleash devastating multi-slash attacks like "Purgatory Onigiri" and the "1080 Pound Phoenix".

King of Hell

By mastering external Haki flow and infusing his swords with Advanced Conqueror's Haki, Zoro has attained the power to cut through anything, earning him the epithet "King of Hell".

The Warrior of Science

Vinsmoke "Black Leg" Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat Crew, born into the scientific assassin Vinsmoke Family of Germa 66. A chivalrous fighter who uses only his legs to protect his hands (a cook's treasures), he seeks the legendary All Blue where fish from all seas converge.

"I'll never kick a woman, even if I die!"

Ifrit Jambe

An evolution of Diable Jambe, Sanji combines his newly awakened exoskeleton with enhanced Armament Haki to create blue flames hotter than magma, capable of incinerating ancient Zoans like Queen.

Germa Genes

After using the Raid Suit, Sanji's dormant genetic enhancements awakened, granting him an exoskeleton tougher than steel and superhuman regeneration, making him nearly indestructible.

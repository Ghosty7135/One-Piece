The Man Who Conquered Death

Brook is the musician of the Straw Hat Pirates and a living skeleton who ate the Yomi Yomi no Mi — a Devil Fruit that allowed his soul to return to his body after death. His dream is to reunite with the whale Laboon, who has been waiting for him at the Reverse Mountain for over fifty years.

"May I see your panties? Yohohoho!"

Soul Solid

Brook's Yomi Yomi no Mi awakened beyond resurrection — he can now channel his soul's energy externally. His signature technique coats his blade in the cold of the underworld itself, flash-freezing anything his sword touches and allowing him to phase his soul out of his body for scouting or escaping capture.

Fencing Master

Brook is an extremely skilled swordsman who wields a shikomizue (sword cane). His attacks are so fast they can strike opponents multiple times before they register being hit — his most famous move, Aubade Coup Droit, delivers dozens of slashes in a fraction of a second.
